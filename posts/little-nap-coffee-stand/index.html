Little Nap Coffee Stand serves freshly roasted coffee

2021.10.15 / 4 mins-ish / claxx moldii

Just across from Yoyogi Park in Shibuya, you’ll find Little Nap Coffee Stand, a tiny coffee shop with a few extra seats outside. It has a laid-back atmosphere and the friendly owner, Daisuke Hamada, will be more than happy to engage in conversation with visitors.

The high-quality beans are roasted on-site, and the menu includes single-origin drip coffee along with the typical espresso-based drinks, which are prepared on a Synesso machine.
